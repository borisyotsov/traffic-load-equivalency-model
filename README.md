# traffic-load-equivalency-model
Mathematical modeling of traffic flow composition based on road traffic counts and derivation of load equivalency factors for flexible pavement design.
Textual and theoretical parts of this work are released under CC BY 4.0 unless otherwise stated.
I am a PhD engineer and a graduate of the University of Architecture, Civil Engineering and Geodesy in Sofia. This is my own report, which I have revised for the purposes of the course. I presented the original version at the 13th Scientific Conference on Transport Infrastructure, held in Nessebar from October 1 to 3, 2020.
